Chika Server—Build a Society Underground.

This is Chika server, a Japanese Minecraft community built around a simple idea:

What if an entire society lived underground?


Lights are hung in the darkness.

Trees are planeted beneath the earth.

Farms begin to grow where sunlight never reaches.

We value cooperation, discussion, and a relaxed pace of play.

Server info

How to join: Please authenticate via Discord.

Rules

  • Value cooperation. If your actions may affect others, speak to them in advance.
  • The use of pirated versions and fly hacks is prohibited.
  • Building on the surface is prohibited.
  • When gathering resources on the surface, do so in a way that does not damage the landscape.
